A Little About me





As a designer and developer, I understand that the perfect user interface should look good and work even better. Every day, I Focus My attention and efforts on front end development and its coding weapons: HTML, CSS and Javascript



With the advent of new and exciting web technologies such as HTML5 and css3, I focus on keeping myself up to date and follow the road of progressive enhancement, helping people live a great browsing experience.

I’ve designed all sorts of projects for all sorts of clients, and the only thing that all my final products had in common is that they had to sell, market and promote.
The only way to achieve this is by creating masterly, finished products that convey professionalism and user satisfaction. I believe that one of my strongest points is that I always put myself not only in my client’s shoes, but also in the shoes of the end user. Every project has its target audience with its own needs, expectations, taste etc.
Every single detail matters - structure, typography, images, proportions, colors, patterns, lines, edges... All should be synchronized to complete the maximum UX.



When I'm not creating beautiful solutions, I am creating beautiful art(I love sketching andpainting), teaching, travelling and spending quality time with my amazing family.
